SCULPTURES

African Sculptures are not just beautiful works of art,but they carry
deep meaning and significance. they tell story of ancestors and deities,
of myth and legends and of the struggles and triumphs of African people.

Let us appreciate and celebrate the beauty and significance of african Sculptures,and let us continue to support and promote the talented artists who keep this tradition alive.
